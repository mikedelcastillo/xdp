Meet AJ
Hi, my name is Ayrel Jay O. Marinduque. I was born in Tagaytay. Like my older brother, Nelord, my childhood was never at its best since I was a product of a broken family. I was not able to go to college. However, my determination and hard work paid off when I was promoted as supervisor of one of the leading grocery stores in our country. A couple of years after, I decided to work abroad. At the age of 26, I was young and healthy until I felt something strange happening to my eyes during my second contract. I was unusually blinking a lot I often had to use my hands to open my eyes just to see normally. My company had to send me back to the Philippines after being diagnosed with blespharopasm. A few days after I got back, my condition worsened and I was diagnosed with cervical dystonia which later turned out to be the beginnings of my journey with XDP.
